Who We Are


RHODECO RUBBER was founded in 1993 and started as an exclusive supplier of rubber compounds for its parent company Maresco Rubber Corporation (founded 1970). Over the years we have expanded our services to other companies in various sectors nationwide and have proven superiority in our workmanship from start to finish. Our growing capabilities have enabled us to service customers of all sizes – from large corporations to individual entrepreneurs.

Today, as an independent company, Rhodeco is one of Philippine’s most reliable source for custom compounding, molding, and fabrication services. In 2012, we acquired a Certification for ISO 9001:2008. Our team takes pride in this great accomplishment which only proves that our products are backed by quality management from order processing down to after-sales support.

SUPERIOR PRODUCTS AND SERVICES FROM START TO FINISH is our promise. Our continued success in serving our customers and suppliers is driven by this commitment. We make sure that this is communicated and understood in all departments. What clearly sets us apart is the dedication and hard work of our employees. By promoting teamwork, recognizing each one’s contributions and holding ourselves accountable for the results, we build a solid team that works together toward this goal.

At Rhodeco Rubber, we strive everyday to find engineering solutions to our clients. It gives us great satisfaction to see our products at work and making people’s lives even better. The success of our customers becomes our success that is why we constantly search for ways to improve our methods to better serve existing customers as well as tap into newer markets. We are proud to have maintained long-term partnerships with numerous satisfied customers and look forward to serving more.
